Developer Documentation

🔌 How does API integration work?
Your app runs on your own server. When a customer installs your app, we send a webhook to notify you. When they use your app, we make API calls to your endpoint with authentication tokens. You respond with data, and we display it in their dashboard.
🔐 How is authentication handled?
We provide JWT tokens with each API call. Verify the token signature using our public key. The token contains: customer_id, unit_id, app_id, and scopes. Never store customer passwords - we handle all authentication.
💾 How does data storage work?
You store all data on your own servers. We provide a customer_id for each installation. Use this ID to associate data with the right customer. We never access your database directly.
📊 How are customers billed?
We handle all billing through Stripe. When a customer subscribes to your app, Stripe charges them and deposits funds directly into your connected Stripe account. For usage-based pricing, report usage via our API and we'll charge accordingly.
🎨 What about the user interface?
You provide a dashboard URL that we embed via iframe in the customer's dashboard. Use responsive design and match our color scheme. We provide CSS variables for easy theming.
📡 What webhooks do you send?
app.installed - When customer installs your app
app.uninstalled - When customer removes your app
subscription.updated - Plan changes
subscription.cancelled - Cancellation
All webhooks include customer_id and app_id.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why 0% fees? What's the catch?
No catch! We're new and need apps to attract customers. Early developers get 0% forever as a thank you. Standard rate will be 20-30% for later developers. Lock in 0% now (limited to first 100 developers).
Can I lose the 0% rate?
Never! Once you're approved as an early developer, you keep 0% fees forever. Even if you add new apps, they all get 0% fees. This is locked in permanently.
What if my app breaks or goes offline?
You're responsible for uptime and support. We monitor app health and will notify you of issues. Repeated outages may result in removal from marketplace. Maintain 99%+ uptime for best results.
Can I change pricing later?
Yes! You can update pricing anytime. Existing customers keep their current rate (grandfathered). New customers get the new rate. We recommend starting lower and raising prices as you add features.
What languages/frameworks can I use?
Any! As long as you can receive HTTP requests and respond with JSON, you're good. Popular choices: Node.js, Python, Ruby, Go, PHP. Use whatever you're comfortable with.
How do I get paid?
Via Stripe Connect. Money flows: Customer → Stripe → Your Bank Account. Payouts are automatic (daily, weekly, or monthly - you choose). We never hold your money.
Can I sell services, not just apps?
Absolutely! AI services, consulting, done-for-you solutions - anything businesses need. As long as you can deliver via API or dashboard integration, it works.
What support do you provide?
Developer Slack channel, email support, documentation, code examples, and quarterly developer calls. Top apps get co-marketing support and featured placement.
