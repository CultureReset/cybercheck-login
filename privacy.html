Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to CyberCheck and all businesses and platforms operating on the CyberCheck platform, including Gulf Coast Radar (GCR) and individual business websites powered by CyberCheck. It explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number provided during booking, signup, or contact form submission
  • Booking details including date, time, and service selections
  • SMS opt-in consent status
  • Travel preferences and interests provided during GCR tourist registration
  • Device and usage data collected automatically (browser type, IP address, pages visited)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm bookings made through CyberCheck-powered websites
  • To send transactional SMS messages including booking confirmations, reminders, and updates — only with your explicit consent
  • To send a welcome SMS and personalized local recommendations to tourists who register on Gulf Coast Radar
  • To notify business owners of new bookings and contact form submissions
  • To respond to inquiries submitted through contact forms
  • To improve platform services and user experience

3. SMS Communications

By providing your phone number and checking the opt-in box during booking or registration, you consent to receive SMS messages. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

SMS messages are sent via Twilio. We do not share your phone number with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your phone number is used solely to deliver the messages you consented to receive.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Twilio — SMS delivery
  • Stripe — Payment processing
  • Supabase — Secure data storage

These providers access your data only as necessary to perform their services and are bound by their own privacy policies. We do not sell or share your personal information with any third party for marketing, advertising, or any other commercial purpose.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
