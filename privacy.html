Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Ghost Marketplace, we believe in radical transparency. This includes how we handle your data. We collect only what we need, store it securely, and never sell your information.

TL;DR: We're not in the data business. We're in the automation business. Your data stays yours.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password
  • Payment Information: Processed by Stripe - we never store card numbers
  • Profile Data: Optional bio, company name, profile picture
  • Content You Create: Apps, workflows, code you build in the sandbox

Automatically Collected:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, tokens consumed
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, device type
  • Log Data: API calls, errors, performance metrics
  • Cookies: Session management and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Process your requests, run your apps, calculate token usage
  • Billing: Charge for token usage and process payments
  • Communication: Send service updates, billing notifications, and support responses
  • Improve the Platform: Analyze usage patterns to make the service better
  • Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, protect user accounts
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to anyone
  • Use your data to train AI models (your code stays yours)
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Track you across other websites

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers:

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • AWS: Cloud infrastructure and hosting
  • OpenAI/Anthropic: AI model API calls (only when you use AI features)
  • Twilio: Voice and SMS services

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

Legal Requirements:

We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights or the safety of others.

Business Transfers:

If Ghost Marketplace is acquired, your data may transfer to the new owner. We'll notify you beforehand.

4. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted 30 days after account deletion
  • Backups: Backup data is deleted within 90 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data (billing records) may be retained longer for legal compliance

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Access:

Request a copy of all data we have about you.

Portability:

Export your data in machine-readable format (JSON) at any time from your dashboard.

Correction:

Update or correct your personal information through your account settings.

Deletion:

Delete your account and all associated data. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

Objection:

Object to certain processing activities. Contact privacy@ghost.app.

How to Exercise Your Rights:

  • Account Settings: profile.html
  • Data Export: Available in account settings
  • Account Deletion: Available in account settings under "Danger Zone"
  • Email Us: privacy@ghost.app

6. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access policies and two-factor authentication
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection
  • Audits: Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Compliance: SOC 2 Type II certified (in progress)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Login sessions, security (required)
  • Preferences: Dark mode, language, dashboard settings
  • Analytics: Understand how you use the platform (can be disabled)

You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

We do NOT use:

  • Third-party advertising cookies
  • Cross-site tracking
  • Social media pixels

8. Third-Party Services

When you connect third-party apps (Stripe, Gmail, CRM, etc.), you're granting those services access to your data according to their privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies.

We only access third-party data with your explicit permission via OAuth.

9. Children's Privacy

Ghost Marketplace is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact privacy@ghost.app.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored in the United States (AWS us-east-1). If you're accessing from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the US.

For EU users: We comply with GDPR requirements for international data transfers.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of material changes via:

  • Email notification
  • In-app notification
  • Notice on this page

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? We're here to help:

  • Privacy Team: privacy@ghost.app
  • Support: Contact Page
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@ghost.app

13. Specific Regional Rights

California Residents (CCPA):

You have the right to know what personal information we collect, use, and share. You can request deletion and opt-out of sale (though we don't sell your data).

EU/UK Residents (GDPR):

You have additional rights including data portability, right to be forgotten, and the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

Other Jurisdictions:

We respect privacy rights in all jurisdictions. Contact us to exercise your rights under local laws.
