Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Power Company model?

We operate like your power company - you only pay for what you use. No monthly subscriptions. We show you exactly what AI services cost us, add our 20% markup, and that's what you pay. Complete transparency.

How much do tokens cost?

Tokens start at $0.001 each. A voice command costs 5 tokens ($0.005), API call costs 2 tokens ($0.002), building an app costs 50-500 tokens. Most users spend $20-50/month. See our pricing page for details.

Do tokens expire?

No! Purchased tokens never expire. Use them whenever you want.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, unused tokens are eligible for refund within 30 days of purchase.

What apps can I connect?

We have 200+ apps including CRM, Gmail, Stripe, Twilio, Slack, and more. Browse our integrations catalog.

How do I build custom apps?

Use our Sandbox editor with three modes: AI Builder (chat to generate code), Visual Builder (drag-and-drop), or Code Editor (write manually). Deploy with one click.

Can I publish apps and make money?

Yes! Publish your apps on our marketplace and earn 70% of revenue. We handle billing, hosting, and support.

Is there an API?

Yes, full REST API with webhooks. See our API documentation.
